Project 01 · Crystal Jewellery & Spiritual Wellness
Aumya
Luxury identity, sacred geometry, and a brand built to feel like a ritual — not an accessory.

Overview

Aumya is a luxury crystal jewellery and spiritual wellness brand built on products that carry intention, beauty, and meaning. The objective was a visual identity that could balance luxury and spirituality without tipping into visual clutter or overused mystical aesthetics.

The Challenge

Spiritual brands often drown in celestial graphics and mandalas; luxury jewellery brands often feel elegant but emotionally disconnected from spirituality. Aumya needed to bridge both worlds while staying sophisticated and timeless.

Strategy — Three Pillars

Elegance
Positions the brand within the premium jewellery market.
Spirituality
Reflects healing, energy, and inner connection.
Timelessness
Ensures longevity beyond short-term trends.

Logo Concept

An Om‑inspired letterform anchors the mark, joined by a sacred bell symbolising vibration and awakening, and a crystal pendant representing healing energy and the brand's core product — together reading as sacred, elegant, and instantly memorable.

Colour Strategy

Cosmic Midnightdepth · mystery · luxury

Sage Mistgrounding · balance

Project 02 · AI Automation · Web Development · Brand Design
AI Jugaad
A technology brand engineered to feel human — innovative without the intimidation.

Overview

AI Jugaad offers AI automation, web development, and brand design solutions. The goal was a brand that feels innovative and professional, while staying approachable and human.

The Challenge

Most AI brands read as cold and technical — dark interfaces, futuristic symbols, complex systems that create distance between businesses and technology. The challenge was making AI feel accessible.

Strategy — Three Pillars

Simplicity
Technology should feel easy to understand.
Creativity
Innovation should feel exciting, not overwhelming.
Human Connection
Communicates collaboration over complexity.

Logo Concept

Simplified letterforms pair with a spark element representing innovation, problem-solving, and possibility. Smooth curves balance the brand's technical services with a friendly personality — "You dream it. We Jugaad it."

Colour Strategy

Mustard Yellowinnovation · problem-solving

Off Whitesimplicity · balance

Project 03 · Family Chinese Restaurant
Tasty Bite
Extra fried noodles. Extra chutney. Extra love. A desi‑Chinese identity built on flavour and familiarity.

Overview

Tasty Bite is a family-focused Chinese restaurant celebrating the Indian love for Indo‑Chinese cuisine. The identity was designed to feel flavourful, nostalgic, and memorable.

The Challenge

Many restaurant brands lean on generic Chinese symbolism, making them visually similar and forgettable. The challenge was building a distinctive identity rooted in food and experience — not stereotypes.

Strategy — Four Pillars

Food
Makes the dish the hero of the visual language.
Flavour
Bold, chatpata experiences.
Family
Warmth and familiarity.
Recall
Instant recognition.

Logo Concept

A bowl, flowing noodle illustration, and premium typography combine for an immediate, appetite‑driven association with the cuisine.

Colour Strategy

Forest Greenpremium positioning
Tasty Redappetite · energy
Noodle Goldrichness · warmth
Chatpata Orangeflavour · excitement

"Tasty Bite became a premium yet approachable desi‑Chinese restaurant brand, built around flavour and familiarity."
Project 04 · Creative Arts & Workshop Brand
Hunar‑e‑Kalaa
Born with Hunar. Made with Kalaa. A creative community brand built for beginners and dreamers alike.

Overview

Hunar‑e‑Kalaa is a creative community brand designed to encourage artistic exploration through workshops, crafts, and hands‑on experiences.

The Challenge

Many people feel intimidated by creative activities, believing they lack artistic skill. The brand needed to feel welcoming, encouraging, and entirely free of judgment.

Strategy — Four Pillars

Comfort
Makes creativity feel approachable.
Exploration
Encourages experimentation.
Community
Builds a sense of belonging.
Playfulness
Creates joy throughout.

Logo Concept

Watercolour textures, handmade illustrations, and soft visual elements come together to communicate creativity without pressure.
